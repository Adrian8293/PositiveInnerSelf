Mental Health

Individual Counseling

One-on-one therapy is at the heart of what we do. Our licensed therapists work with you to understand your unique concerns, set meaningful goals, and build the tools you need to thrive. Sessions are warm, evidence-based, and entirely tailored to you.

We specialize in: anxiety, depression, trauma & PTSD, grief, life transitions, ADHD, OCD, substance use, relationship concerns, and more. All sessions are LGBTQIA+ affirming and trauma-informed.

Therapeutic approaches include: CBT, DBT, EMDR, ACT, mindfulness, somatic therapy, narrative therapy, and the Nurtured Heart Approach.

Community Healing

Group Counseling & Workshops

Healing in community is powerful. Our therapeutic group sessions bring people together around shared experiences — reducing isolation while building skills and connection. Groups are facilitated by our licensed clinicians in a safe, structured environment.

Current & upcoming groups cover: anxiety management, grief support, relationship skills, DBT skills training, and wellness workshops.

Groups are a powerful complement to individual therapy and are often covered by insurance. Contact us to learn about current group availability and schedules.

Relationships

Family & Couples Counseling

Strong families and healthy relationships are built with intention. Our clinicians specialize in family systems therapy, couples counseling, and parent training to help you improve communication, repair ruptures, and build stronger bonds.

We work with: couples navigating conflict or intimacy challenges, families with children and adolescents, blended families, parents seeking guidance, and co-parenting arrangements.

Several of our providers also specialize in foster care, adoption, and attachment — bringing deep expertise to complex family dynamics.

Naturopathic Medicine

Naturopathic & Integrative Medicine

Dr. Vileka Fisher, ND brings the power of naturopathic medicine to our holistic wellness team. Naturopathic medicine integrates evidence-based natural therapies with conventional diagnostics to treat the whole person — body, mind, and spirit.

Services include: women's health & OB/GYN care (IUD insertion/removal, antepartum & postpartum care, colposcopy), preventive health screenings, vaccine administration, urine pregnancy testing, and comprehensive wellness consultations.

Naturopathic services are provided by Dr. Fisher and may be covered by your insurance. See our fees page for CPT codes and pricing.

Body & Mind

Massage Therapy

Our licensed massage therapists provide expert bodywork that supports physical wellness alongside your mental health care. We offer a range of therapeutic techniques in a calm, welcoming environment.

Modalities offered:

  • Bowenwork — gentle yet powerful for musculoskeletal pain, migraines, anxiety, and digestive concerns (Erin Shimp)
  • Deep Tissue — targets chronic muscle tension and pain
  • Swedish / Relaxation — stress relief and nervous system reset
  • Auto Accident Recovery — specialized rehabilitation massage

Innovative Wellness

BioCharger™ Sessions

Revitalize your body and mind with the BioCharger™ — an innovative wellness device that restores your natural energy balance and enhances cellular function. Offered by our massage therapist Erin Shimp.

The BioCharger™ combines four distinct energy types — light therapy (using noble gases like xenon and argon), pulsed electromagnetic fields (PEMF), voltage therapy, and healing frequencies — to support whole-body rejuvenation.

Each 15-minute session can improve mood, reduce pain, ease chronic discomfort, and support physical, mental, and emotional recovery. Non-invasive and gentle.
