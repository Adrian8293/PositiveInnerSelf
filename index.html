Our Mission

We want you to feel heard, respected & supported.

At Positive Inner Self, LLC, we believe true healing comes through a holistic approach. Through counseling, naturopathic medicine, massage therapy, and innovative wellness services, we offer compassionate care that meets you where you are — whether in person or via telehealth.

We believe every person deserves kindness, respect, and support, no matter their race, profession, identity, or personal challenges. We are proudly committed to fostering a welcoming space for everyone, including the LGBTQIA+ community.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first appointment.

We work with select insurance providers including Aetna, BCBS Oregon, OHP/Medicaid, Cigna, and Providence. Please contact us with your insurance information and we will verify your coverage before your first appointment. We also offer a sliding scale fee for those experiencing financial difficulty.
We ask that you provide at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. Late cancellations and no-shows may be subject to a fee. We understand that emergencies happen and will work with you on a case-by-case basis.
Yes! We offer secure telehealth sessions via a HIPAA-compliant platform. Telehealth sessions are available for counseling, supervision, and consultation services across Oregon. You will receive a link to join your session via email before your appointment.
We encourage you to browse our Meet Our Providers page to learn about each clinician's background, specialties, and approach. If you're unsure, simply reach out through our contact form and we'll help match you with the right provider for your needs.
Yes. Confidentiality is a cornerstone of the therapeutic relationship. What you share in sessions is protected by law. There are a few legally mandated exceptions — such as imminent risk of harm — which your provider will explain fully during your first appointment.
Absolutely. We are a proudly LGBTQIA+ affirming practice. All of our providers are committed to providing respectful, affirming, and culturally sensitive care to individuals of all identities, orientations, and backgrounds. You are welcome here exactly as you are.
We accept major credit and debit cards, HSA/FSA cards, and certain insurance plans. Payment is due at the time of service. Please contact us if you have questions about payment options or need to discuss a sliding scale arrangement.
Your first session is an intake appointment where you and your provider will get to know each other. Your provider will ask about your history, current concerns, and goals for therapy.
