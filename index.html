Our Mission

We want you to feel heard, respected & supported.

At Positive Inner Self, LLC, we believe true healing comes through a holistic approach. Through counseling and consultation, we offer compassionate care that meets you where you are — whether in person or via telehealth.

We believe that every person deserves kindness, respect, and support, no matter their race, profession, identity, or personal challenges. We are committed to fostering a welcoming and secure environment for everyone, including the LGBTQIA+ community.
