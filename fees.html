Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept insurance?
+

We work with select insurance providers. Please contact us with your insurance information and we will verify your coverage before your first appointment. We also offer a sliding scale fee for those experiencing financial difficulty.

What is your cancellation policy?
+

We ask that you provide at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. Late cancellations and no-shows may be subject to a fee. We understand that emergencies happen and will work with you on a case-by-case basis.

Do you offer telehealth sessions?
+

Yes! We offer secure telehealth sessions via a HIPAA-compliant platform. Telehealth sessions are available for counseling, supervision, and consultation services. You will receive a link to join your session via email before your appointment.

How do I know which provider is right for me?
+

We encourage you to browse our Meet Our Providers page to learn about each clinician's background, specialties, and approach. If you're unsure, simply reach out through our contact form and we'll help match you with the right provider for your needs.

Is what I share in therapy confidential?
+

Yes. Confidentiality is a cornerstone of the therapeutic relationship. What you share in sessions is protected by law. There are a few legally mandated exceptions — such as imminent risk of harm — which your provider will explain fully during your first appointment.

What should I expect during my first appointment?
+

Your first session is an intake appointment where you and your provider will get to know each other. Your provider will ask about your history, current concerns, and goals for therapy. This is also a chance for you to ask any questions you have. There's no pressure — first sessions are about building comfort and connection.

Do you offer services for children and adolescents?
+

Several of our providers specialize in working with children, adolescents, and young adults. Please contact us or check our providers' bios to see who works with your age group.

Are you affirming of LGBTQIA+ clients?
+

Absolutely. We are a proudly LGBTQIA+ affirming practice. All of our providers are committed to providing respectful, affirming, and culturally sensitive care to individuals of all identities, orientations, and backgrounds. You are welcome here exactly as you are.

How long does therapy typically last?
+

The length of therapy varies depending on your goals and needs. Some people find a few sessions helpful for a specific challenge, while others benefit from ongoing support over many months or years. Your provider will regularly check in with you about your progress and goals to ensure your treatment plan is working for you.

What forms of payment do you accept?
+

We accept major credit and debit cards, HSA/FSA cards, and certain insurance plans. Payment is due at the time of service. Please contact us if you have questions about payment options or need to discuss a sliding scale arrangement.

Still Have Questions?

We're happy to answer any questions before you schedule your first appointment. Reach out anytime.
