About Jessica

My name is Jessica Padnick and I'm a Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW) in both Oregon and California. I am a mental health clinician at Positive Inner Self as well as a Certified Alcohol and Drug Counselor I (CADCI). I work with adult individuals and couples to address anxiety, depression, trauma, and relationship concerns.

I want to collaborate with you to increase mindful awareness of what is or is not working in your life, explore core values, and work together toward your goals. Together we can work on practical strategies to address current issues while also exploring and addressing the root causes of underlying symptoms.

I strive to provide a safe, non-judgmental space to identify and compassionately challenge patterns and dynamics that are not serving you. If this approach could be a good fit for your needs, please reach out — I look forward to meeting you.

Background & Experience

With dual licensure in Oregon and California and certification as an Alcohol and Drug Counselor, Jessica brings a broad and experienced perspective to mental health care. She specializes in working with adults and couples navigating anxiety, depression, trauma, and relationship challenges.

What to Expect

We will begin by reviewing your presenting concerns and exploring what's bringing you to therapy. Together we'll identify goals and develop strategies that work for your unique situation, all within a safe, collaborative, and non-judgmental space.
