LCSW

I am Emilee, a Licensed Clinical Social Worker.

In times of struggle, reaching out for help is very challenging. You've taken the first step and I'm glad you're here. My approach is client-centered, utilizing evidence-based practices of Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Mindfulness, and Problem-Solving to help you process and move through the seasons of depression, anxiety, interpersonal conflict, vocational burnout, or adjust to life with a new diagnosis.

I have been working in the social work field for 14 years. A significant portion of that time was spent working as a medical social worker in both inpatient and outpatient settings. I'm uniquely attuned to the vocational stress experienced on a daily basis by healthcare workers and others — but I don't limit my practice to any one group. Adult individuals ages 18 and up from all walks of life are welcome and accepted.

Therapy is a wonderful tool that can reduce stress and improve function in daily life. I have confidence in your capacity to determine what will bring meaning and peace to your life, identify barriers, and create solutions.

Areas of Focus

Background & Experience

14 years in social work with significant experience as a medical social worker in both inpatient and outpatient healthcare settings. Uniquely skilled at supporting healthcare professionals and others experiencing vocational burnout.

What to Expect

You'll find a warm, non-judgmental space where we'll work together at your pace. I'm hopeful that together we can work towards your individual goals, building practical strategies alongside deeper understanding.
