About Adrienne

Hi there, I'm Adrienne Buhacoff, Owner and Clinic Director at Positive Inner Self, and a Licensed Clinical Social Worker in Oregon (L11095). I specialize in supporting individuals and families navigating anxiety, depression, trauma and abuse, grief and loss, and life transitions.

I also focus on foster care and adoption, infant and toddler mental health, attachment issues, and parent training using The Nurtured Heart Approach. My therapy style is trauma-informed, holistic, systemic, and rooted in anti-oppression values.

I'm proudly LGBTQIA+ affirming, creating a safe and welcoming space where all identities are respected and valued. I truly believe we're all doing the best we can with the tools we have. I love exploring and finding your strengths, supporting you in using them to create positive change in your life and for your family.

Background & Experience

I have worked with children, teens, and their families for 18+ years. My career path began with adolescents and teens with substance abuse issues, providing individual and group therapy as well as parenting classes.

I have also worked with children (ages 0–12) and their caregivers in the child welfare system (foster care, adoption, and reunification) working through difficulties surrounding trauma responses, attachment difficulties, anxiety, depression, and ADHD. More recently, I provided intensive, community-based therapy services to teens and their families in crisis.

I am also a mother of four children, which informs my deep empathy for families navigating the beautiful, complex work of raising kids.

What to Expect

Initially, we will meet to discuss your concerns and ensure that I am a good fit for your needs. We will review the presenting issues and develop a treatment plan that meets your and your family's needs. I enjoy using humor and playfulness to create a relaxed environment where real change can happen.
