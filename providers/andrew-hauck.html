I am Andrew, a Professional Counselor Associate.

I am Andrew Hauck, a Professional Counselor Associate with a Master of Science degree, practicing at Positive Inner Self. I bring a science-informed, client-centered approach to counseling, passionate about helping individuals understand themselves more deeply and create positive, lasting change.

I believe in the capacity of every person to grow and heal when given the right support. My approach is collaborative and evidence-based, always adapted to your unique needs and goals.
